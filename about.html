About Us

About us

An initiative by the department of computer science engineering faculties and students (enrolled in IBM collaboration programme) to conduct technical, non-technical and awareness events as online and offline staying at home during COVID-19 lockdown. This event focuses on admission strategy, awareness, exposure of talents, being active and have fun during lockdown, promotion and branding. There are totally 8 events where 5 events are talent contest which will be conducted for students and 2 events are COVID-19 awareness which will be conducted for all age groups.
