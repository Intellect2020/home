General Rules

Participant Categories:

Category 1 : School students ( 9th – 12th standard)

Category 2 : Diploma & Undergraduate college students

Category 3 : Open to all ages

1. Participation at any time from home and submit their work through email.

2. Registration ends on 15.05.2020 and submission ends on 17.05.2020.

3. Talent contest events are only for students (category1 & 2).

4. Details of the events is available at tiny.cc/intellect2k20

5. Winners and prizes are awarded only for talent contest events (students).

6. Separate winner and prizes for category 1 and category 2 in talent contest events.

7. Registration is mandatory for all events.

8. E-certificate will be provided for all the registered participants.
